Upon returning home from this initial encounter, she immediately shared her suspicions with me, explicitly wondering whether they might be associated with U.S. intelligence. At the time, I completely dismissed her suspicions, believing instead that they were merely Christian missionaries. My wife, being a Chinese citizen, was meeting Christian missionaries for the first time in her life, which understandably raised her suspicions. However, having grown up in a Catholic family and having previously encountered missionaries, I personally found their presence and activities perfectly normal.
