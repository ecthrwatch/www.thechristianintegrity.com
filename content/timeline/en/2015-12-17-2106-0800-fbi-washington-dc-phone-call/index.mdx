On December 17, 2015, I contacted the Washington D.C. FBI Field Office at +1-202-278-2000 to report the crimes committed by McDonald’s Corporation and their accomplices. The conversation lasted approximately 6 minutes and 35 seconds. Upon disclosing my location in China to the phone operator/intake agent, I was informed that the FBI could not take action as they lacked jurisdiction in China. However, this response seems to overlook the extraterritorial application of many American laws, suggesting a potential oversight in handling my report.
