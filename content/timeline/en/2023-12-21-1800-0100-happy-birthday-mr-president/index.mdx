Mr. President,
 
Exactly five years ago to the day, on December 21, 2018, I registered the domain name [Macron-Emmanuel.com](https://www.Macron-Emmanuel.com) to launch an unofficial website about you, Mr. President.
 
My intention has always been benevolent. As I have done for other high-level officials and magistrates such as [Nicole Belloubet](https://www.Nicole-Belloubet.com/open-letters/), [Cédric Villani](https://www.CedricVillani.com/), or [François Molins](https://www.FrancoisMolins.com), my aim was to raise public awareness not only of the serious crimes committed by the transnational criminal entity McDonald's Corporation but also to highlight the recurring pattern of crimes of forgery and use of forged documents within the French judicial system.
 
Here is one of the many times I attempted to alert the former Minister of Justice, Nicole Belloubet:
 
https://www.nicole-belloubet.com/open-letters/french-minister-of-justice-nicole-belloubet-notified-of-forgery-in-the-mcdonalds-case-2018-10-23/
 
I prove that the date was altered, not only beyond a reasonable doubt but without a shadow of a doubt, even though the burden of proof should have normally fallen on the Versailles High Court (Tribunal de Grande Instance de Versailles).
 
Since 2018, there have been several dramatic developments.
 
Although I need to publish more documents online, I hope, as President of France, you will be extremely diligent and vigilant about the communications I am about to send you very soon.
 
Meanwhile, as today is your birthday, allow me to wish you a happy birthday, Mr. President.
 
I also wish you a Merry Christmas and happy holiday season.
 
Sincerely,
 
Vincent B. Le Corre
