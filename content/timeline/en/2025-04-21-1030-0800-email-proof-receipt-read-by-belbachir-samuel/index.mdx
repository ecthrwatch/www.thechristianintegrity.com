### 📌 **Why this email read receipt matters**

An official email reporting a **serious and substantiated threat** was sent on **April 19, 2025**, to multiple high-ranking French and Chinese authorities, including a French senator, diplomats, intelligence services, and journalists. The email contained an **[open letter addressed to Pierre Mirochnikoff](https://www.ecthrwatch.org/timeline/mortal-danger-in-beijing-china-open-letter-to-pierre-mirochnikoff-2025-04-19/)**, President of the Union of French Citizens Abroad, urging the transmission of my alerts to the French diplomatic post.

Although **[Samuel Belbachir](https://www.ecthrwatch.org/tags/samuel-belbachir/)**, an official at the **French Ministry of Foreign Affairs**, was neither a direct recipient nor copied on the message, a **read receipt confirms** that he accessed it on **April 21 at 10:30 a.m.** This indicates that **someone internally must have forwarded him the message**, most likely as part of the administrative or diplomatic handling of the case.
