The McDonald’s case, which the U.S. Department of Justice knew, or ought to have known about since at least the summer/fall of 2019, involves serious criminal activities by McDonald’s Corporation and its accomplices. This case demands urgent transparency due to its significant public interest and profound global impact.

The repercussions of these activities have been profoundly detrimental worldwide, highlighting serious failings in both the French and American judicial systems.
