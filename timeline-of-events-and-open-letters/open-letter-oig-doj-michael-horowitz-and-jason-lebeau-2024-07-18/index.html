Dear Mr. Horowitz and Mr. LeBeau,

I have published this communication as an open letter on Michael-Evan-Horowitz.com, a non-official website on you Mr. Horowitz, and on Jason-LeBeau.dojoig.com, a non-official website on you Mr. LeBeau.

I kindly request your understanding and patience with the methods I have employed. The McDonald’s case, which the U.S. Department of Justice knew, or ought to have known about since at least the summer/fall of 2019, involves serious criminal activities by McDonald’s Corporation and its accomplices. This case demands urgent transparency due to its significant public interest and profound global impact.

The repercussions of these activities have been profoundly detrimental worldwide, highlighting serious failings in both the French and American judicial systems.

I am currently subjected to what can only be described as an informal gag order, which could be construed as a form of blackmail, witness tampering, obstruction of justice, and could even border on extortion. This situation is wholly unacceptable, and you may not be fully aware of its extent. However, I will be providing you with a full disclosure shortly.
